Recycling Programs

Kenya has implemented various recycling programs aimed at reducing landfill waste and promoting the reuse of materials such as plastics, paper, and metals. Community participation is key to the success of these programs.

Waste Reduction

Efforts to reduce waste generation include promoting the use of biodegradable materials, encouraging composting, and raising awareness about the environmental impact of excessive waste.

Composting Initiatives

Composting organic waste helps reduce landfill volume and produces valuable fertilizer for agriculture and gardening.

Government Initiatives

The Kenyan government supports waste management through policies, regulations, and funding for infrastructure such as waste collection and treatment facilities.

Environmental Policies

Policies focus on sustainable waste management practices, enforcement of anti-littering laws, and promotion of public-private partnerships.
